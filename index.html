Our Story

Established in 1953

Olivia's Table began in the charming coastal village of Porto Azzurro, Italy, as a modest trattoria with a big heart. Founded by the passionate and gifted cook, Olivia Rossi, the restaurant quickly became known for transforming humble, local ingredients into culinary treasures.

In the early 1950s, Olivia brought her legacy to America‚Äîalongside family recipes, a philosophy of warmth, and the belief that food should not only nourish the body but also the soul.

From its early days as a ten-table eatery, Olivia's commitment to seasonal freshness and heartfelt hospitality earned loyal guests from near and far. Today, three generations later, her spirit lives on in every dish served.
